Partner With Future Shock與 Future Shock 攜手

Every contribution drives us forward. Our sponsorship structure is inspired by the natural hierarchy of bear species, ranked by size — from the nimble Sun Bear to the massive Polar Bear. As the bear grows in stature, so does the reach and influence of your brand within the FIRST Robotics community. Sponsors gain visibility at competitions, outreach events reaching 12,500+ people a season, and a direct role in cultivating Taiwan's next generation of engineers. 每一份支持都推動我們前進。我們的贊助方案以熊科動物的體型階層為靈感——從敏捷的馬來熊到巨大的北極熊。熊的體型越大，象徵您的品牌在 FIRST 機器人社群中的影響力越廣。贊助者可在比賽與每季觸及超過 12,500 人的推廣活動中曝光，並直接參與培育台灣下一代工程人才。
