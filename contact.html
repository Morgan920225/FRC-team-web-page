🎓 Students — Join the team🎓 學生——加入我們

We recruit Mingdao students from junior high through senior high (grades 8–11 currently on the roster). No experience needed — our Mechanical, Programming, and Administrative training programs take you from zero to competition-ready, taught by alumni coaches. Watch for recruitment announcements at school and on Instagram. 我們招收明道中學國中部到高中部的學生（現役隊員涵蓋國二至高二）。不需任何經驗——機構、程式與行政培訓課程由校友教練授課，帶你從零到能上場比賽。請留意校內公告與 Instagram 的招生訊息。

🤝 Sponsors — Invest in the future🤝 贊助者——投資未來

Email us for our sponsorship deck and a visit to our Future Lab workspace. See the bear-tier program for benefits at each level, from NT$5,000 to NT$100,000+. 歡迎來信索取贊助簡報並參訪 Future Lab 工作空間。各級回饋請見熊級贊助方案，從 NT$5,000 到 NT$100,000 以上。

🤖 FRC teams — Exchange & Future Help🤖 FRC 隊伍——交流與 Future Help

Rookie or developing team? Our Future Help program offers mentorship, field access, hardware, and fundraising guidance. Established teams: we host and join exchanges year-round, plus the annual Taiwan FRC Summit. 新創或發展中的隊伍？Future Help 計畫提供技術指導、場地、硬體與募資協助。成熟隊伍：我們全年舉辦並參與交流活動，還有年度台灣 FRC 高峰會。

👪 Parents — See what your kids build👪 家長——看見孩子的成長

Our Season Showcase, Robot Reveal, and Annual Season Review are open to parents — come see the robot, meet the mentors, and watch your child present engineering work to a real audience. 賽季成果展、機器人發表會與年度回顧都歡迎家長參加——來看看機器人、認識導師，見證孩子向真實觀眾發表工程成果。
