2018 · FIRST POWER UP

Season story賽季故事

Our rookie season. With 5 mentors and 50 students selected from 200+ applicants, we built our first robot in the FAB LAB and took it to the Southern Cross Regional — Rank 29, 2nd pick of Alliance 2 — bringing home the Imagery Award in honor of Jack Kamen.創隊首季。5 位導師與從 200 多位報名者中選出的 50 名學生，在 FAB LAB 打造第一台機器人，遠征 Southern Cross 區域賽——排名 29、第 2 聯盟第二指名——並抱回 Imagery Award（Jack Kamen 紀念獎）。

🏆 Imagery Award in honor of Jack Kamen (Southern Cross Regional)
