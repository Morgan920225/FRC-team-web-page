2025 · REEFSCAPE

Season story賽季故事

A comeback season. We earned the Rising All-Star Award at the New Taipei City Regional (Rank 20), then — as one of only two foreign teams — recovered from a catastrophic elevator collapse at the Oklahoma Regional to win the Excellence in Engineering Award, a tribute to our modular design philosophy. The Taiwan Playoff brought the AMD Impact & Inspiration Award.強勢回歸的一季。我們在新北區域賽獲 Rising All-Star 獎（排名 20）；接著以僅有的兩支外國隊伍之一出戰奧克拉荷馬區域賽，從升降機構全面崩壞中迅速搶修重返賽場，贏得卓越工程獎——這是對模組化設計理念的最佳肯定。台灣季後賽再獲 AMD Impact & Inspiration 獎。

🏆 Rising All-Star Award (New Taipei City Regional)🏆 Excellence in Engineering Award (Oklahoma Regional)🏆 AMD Impact & Inspiration Award (Taiwan Playoff)
